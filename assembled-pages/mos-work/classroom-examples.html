Queen Anne’s County High School
Business Program

About ten years ago, Maryland partnered with Certiport to make MOS
exams available in all high schools.

Queen Anne’s County High School is one example of success. The first year,
the school had five students certify. The second year, MOS certification
grew to 25 students. This high school now certifies 85 to 100 students
every year. Many of the school’s MOS students have won prizes at the
national MOS championships and qualified to compete in the annual
World Championships.

administrators, and students think.
