MOS Competitions

Put your skills to the test. Become the best.

Rise Above

Certiport’s Microsoft Office Specialist World Championship tests students’
skills on Microsoft Office Word, Excel® and PowerPoint®. We invite top
students to represent their countries at the World Championship.

In some countries or regions, students are required to participate in a
national championship, and the winners continue on to the World
Championship. In the U.S. alone, more than 372,000 students recently
qualified to compete, and 87 made it to the finals. With certified skills, all
students are—and will continue to be—winners.

Get certified to enter. This year, the World Championships will be held in
Orlando, Florida, August 7–10. Winners can receive up to $7,500 in prize
money, trophies, medals, certificates, and other prizes provided by
Microsoft.

How far will you go? What prizes will you earn? You will never know until
you certify and try.
