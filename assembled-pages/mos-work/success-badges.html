Show Off Your Success

MOS-certified students have a lot to be proud of.
Badges validate their skills and allow them to show the
world what they’ve accomplished.

STAND OUT. BE SEEN.

It’s hard to stand out from the crowd. The same degree produces different workers with varying levels of
talent. The same job title can mean 10 different things. Looking good on paper is different from being
good in practice. How can a school or employer tell the difference when deciding who to admit or hire?

When you earn a professionally recognized digital badge for MOS certification, you can proudly display it
in your résumé, in emails, or on your social media profiles. The badge broadcasts your skill, sets you
apart, and brands you as a candidate ready to hit the ground running.
