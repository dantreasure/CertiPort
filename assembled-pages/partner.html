Partner with Certiport

Partnership with Certiport gives organizations
the ability to promote and distribute Microsoft
Office Specialist certification exams.

CERTIPORT AUTHORIZED PARTNER PROGRAM

A Certiport Authorized Partner
(CAP) is any commercial institution
that acts as a "purveyor or
distributor" of Certiport exams.
This can be a company, solution
provider, or reseller with access to
a channel of test centers capable
of delivering Certiport exams.

A CATC is any commercial institution that delivers and administers
computer-based Certiport-managed certification exams.

It's free, it's easy, and it's highly beneficial to become a Certiport
Authorized Testing Center.

Certiport Authorized Testing Centers (CATC)

Interact directly
with candidates,
offer valuable
credentials, and add
new certifications.

offering Certiport

Courseware Partners

Courseware publishers and training material providers can partner with
Certiport to promote the Microsoft Office Specialist programs. Whether
you deliver training to your customers as one of your core services or as
your entire business practice, attaining certification competency can open
new opportunities for your business.

Becoming a Courseware Partner can help you save time and money while
you strengthen your capabilities, better serve customers, and build
connections to reach your full business potential.
